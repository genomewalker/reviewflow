Generate contextual draft responses using Claude or GPT. Get multiple response strategies for each comment.

Expert Panel

Virtual experts discuss complex technical comments, providing diverse perspectives and recommendations.

Workflow

How It Works

Get from reviewer comments to polished responses in four simple steps.

1

Import Your Reviews

Paste reviewer comments directly, upload a PDF/DOCX, or import from a structured JSON file. ReviewFlow automatically parses and organizes comments by reviewer.

2

Process with AI

Click "Process Paper" to analyze all comments. The AI categorizes each comment, identifies key concerns, and prepares expert discussions with potential solutions.

3

Review & Refine

Browse AI-generated responses, select from different strategies, and edit to match your voice. Use the expert panel for complex technical questions.

4

Export & Submit

Export all responses to a formatted Word document. Copy individual responses or download the complete response letter for journal submission.
